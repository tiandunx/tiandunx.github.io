About Me (CV)

Now I'll shift my research direction to the field of autonomous driving.

Before that, I worked at Alibaba DAMO Academy CV lab of JDAI lead by Prof. Tao Mei .

I received my M.E. degree in 2016 from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Stan Z. Li and Prof. Zhen Lei and B.E. from Yanshan University, China in 2013.

My research areas include effective computing, data condensation, autonomous driving, face analysis, noisy robust learning and so on.
